Contacting us

Mailing lists

There are three mailing lists for ASC:

asc-hq-announce

New versions of ASC, unit sets and other major additions to ASC will be announced here:

Click here to subscribe to ASC-hq-announce

There is also an email-based interface for users of this list; you can get info about using it by sending a message with just the word help as subject or in the body, to:
asc-hq-announce-request@lists.sourceforge.net

asc_general

Everybody who wants to participate in email games, discuss new maps, features, the project in general or anything else should join the list.

To subscribe to ASC_general, write a mail to ASC_general-subscribe@onelist.com

or simply click here:

But since you are asked some unnecessary personal questions by egroups (who have bought onelist) when using the web interface, I recommend to subscribe by email

asc_development

This list is intended to hold the discussions for the programmers that work on ASC since I imagine that the stuff here is quite uninteresting to non-programmers.

To subscribe to ASC_development, write a mail to ASC_development-subscribe@onelist.com

or simply click here:

But since you are asked some unnecessary personal questions by egroups (who have bought onelist) when using the web interface, I recommend to subscribe by email
